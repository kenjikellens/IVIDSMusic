# Future Plans - IVIDS Music

This document outlines the vision and upcoming feature ideas for the IVIDS Music application. Our goal is to create the most premium, high-fidelity music experience across Web, Mobile, and TV.

## 🎵 Audio & Playback Enhancements
- **Dynamic Lyrics (LRC Support)**: Implement real-time, synchronized scrolling lyrics with a glassmorphism highlight effect.
- **Pro Audio Equalizer**: 10-band manual equalizer with professional presets (Bass Boost, Vocal, Electronic, etc.).
- **Gapless Playback**: Ensure seamless transitions between tracks for a continuous listening experience.
- **Audio Visualizer**: High-performance, stunning canvas-based visualizers that react to the music's frequency.

## 🎨 UI & UX Evolution
- **Theme Engine**: Allow users to fully customize their experience with custom glassmorphism colors and gradient backdrops.
- **Micro-Animations**: Enhance the feel of the app with subtle physics-based animations for transitions and button interactions.
- **Enhanced TV Interface**: Further optimize the Leanback experience with better grid layouts and remote shortcut support.
- **Mini-Player Redesign**: A floating, interactive mini-player that provides quick access to controls from any page.

## 📊 Discovery & Intelligence
- **Smart Mixes**: AI-powered "Daily Mix" playlists generated based on the user's specific listening history and mood.
- **Mood Explorer**: A dedicated section to browse music by feeling (e.g., Chill, Focus, Workout, Late Night).
- **Listening Insights**: Detailed statistics for the user (Top Artists of the Month, Genres, Total Listening Time).

## 📱 Connectivity & Ecosystem
- **Cross-Device Sync**: Synchronize the "Library" and current "Now Playing" state across Web, Mobile, and Android TV.
- **Social Sharing**: One-click sharing of tracks or playlists with custom-styled preview cards.
- **Sleep Timer**: A customizable timer that gracefully fades out the volume when the time is up.
- **Cast Support**: Integration for casting audio to Chromecast or other smart speakers.

## 🛠️ Performance & Infrastructure
- **Offline Pro**: More robust offline management with the ability to choose download quality (128kbps vs 320kbps).
- **Service Worker Caching**: Improved PWA support for instant loading even in low-connectivity scenarios.
- **Internal Database Migration**: Move from localStorage to IndexedDB for larger, more efficient guest library handling.
